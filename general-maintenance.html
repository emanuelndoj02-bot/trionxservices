General Maintenance

Commercial Handyman & General Maintenance

TrionX provides comprehensive general maintenance services for commercial properties throughout Florida. From drywall repairs and painting to door hardware and ceiling tiles, we handle the everyday maintenance needs that keep your facility looking professional and operating safely.

General maintenance requests are often the most frequent work orders a property generates. We respond quickly and close out work orders with complete documentation.

  • Drywall repair and patching
  • Interior and exterior painting
  • Ceiling tile replacement
  • Door and lock hardware
  • Cabinet and countertop repair
  • Caulking and weatherproofing
  • Pressure washing
  • General handyman services
