About TrionX

Commercial Maintenance, Done Right

TrionX is a commercial facility maintenance company headquartered in Miami, Florida. We provide multi-trade maintenance services for commercial properties throughout Florida, including retail locations, restaurants, office buildings, and industrial facilities.

Our operations are built around the standards that national facility management companies require: guaranteed response times, IVR check-in and check-out, detailed photo documentation, and full compliance with insurance, licensing, and background check requirements.

We handle the work directly — one company, one point of contact, complete accountability from dispatch through completion.

What Sets Us Apart

SLA-Driven Response

We dispatch within agreed service windows and confirm technician arrival through automated IVR check-in. Clients receive real-time status updates throughout every work order.

Complete Documentation

Every service call includes before-and-after photos, detailed technician notes, material lists, and digital completion reports uploaded the same day.

Multi-Trade Capability

Six core trades under one roof means fewer vendor relationships to manage. One call, one company — electrical, plumbing, glass, refrigeration, lighting, and general maintenance.

Vendor Compliance

We maintain current COIs, W-9s, background checks, and licensing documentation. Our compliance files are always audit-ready for national FM platforms.

Licensed & Insured

All work is performed by licensed, insured, and background-checked technicians. We carry general liability and workers' compensation coverage that meets or exceeds industry requirements.

Local Expertise

Based in downtown Miami with deep knowledge of Florida's building codes, climate considerations, and commercial property requirements.
