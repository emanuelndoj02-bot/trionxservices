Lighting Maintenance

Commercial Lighting Services

TrionX provides interior and exterior lighting maintenance for commercial facilities across Florida. From ballast replacements and LED retrofits to parking lot lighting and emergency exit signage, we keep your property safe, visible, and code-compliant.

Proper lighting is essential for safety, customer experience, and regulatory compliance. We handle both reactive repairs and scheduled maintenance programs.

  • Ballast and driver replacement
  • LED retrofit and conversion
  • Parking lot and exterior pole lighting
  • Emergency and exit light maintenance
  • Tenant improvement lighting
  • Dimmer and control installation
  • Lamp and fixture replacement

Why Choose TrionX for Lighting

Energy Savings

LED retrofits can reduce lighting energy costs by 50-70%. We help property managers plan and execute conversion projects that pay for themselves.

Code Compliance

Emergency lights and exit signs have specific testing and maintenance requirements. We maintain records that satisfy fire marshal and building code inspections.

Scheduled Programs

Bulk re-lamping and routine inspections prevent dark spots and safety hazards. We coordinate after-hours work to avoid disrupting business operations.
