Commercial Lighting Maintenance

TrionX delivers comprehensive lighting maintenance services for commercial properties including retail stores, office buildings, restaurants, warehouses, and parking facilities. From LED retrofits to emergency light compliance, we keep your spaces well-lit, safe, and energy efficient.

  • Ballast & LED driver replacements
  • Exterior & parking lot lighting maintenance
