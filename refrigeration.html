Refrigeration Services

Commercial Refrigeration Maintenance

TrionX provides commercial refrigeration services for restaurants, grocery stores, convenience stores, and food service operations throughout Florida. We handle walk-in coolers, reach-in units, ice machines, and prep tables to keep your operation running and compliant.

Refrigeration failures put inventory at risk. We respond within SLA windows and carry common replacement parts to minimize downtime.

  • Walk-in cooler and freezer repair
  • Reach-in unit maintenance and repair
  • Ice machine service and cleaning
  • Compressor diagnostics and replacement
  • Temperature monitoring and calibration
  • Condenser and evaporator coil cleaning
  • Door gasket and hardware replacement

Why Choose TrionX for Refrigeration

Rapid Response

Refrigeration failures can result in thousands of dollars in spoiled inventory. We prioritize refrigeration calls and carry common parts on our service vehicles.

Preventive Maintenance

Regular coil cleaning, gasket inspection, and temperature calibration extend equipment life and prevent emergency failures. We set up recurring maintenance schedules.

Health Code Compliance

Proper refrigeration is a health department requirement. Our documentation includes temperature readings, repair details, and compliance notes for your records.
