Commercial Refrigeration Services

TrionX provides dependable commercial refrigeration repair and maintenance for restaurants, retail stores, grocery outlets, and multi-site businesses. From walk-in coolers to reach-in display cases, our technicians diagnose issues quickly and restore your equipment to peak performance.

  • Walk-in cooler & freezer repairs
  • Reach-in refrigerator & freezer servicing
  • Compressor & condenser maintenance
  • Evaporator coil cleaning & replacement
  • Temperature monitoring & controls
