What We Do

Complete Facility Maintenance. One Reliable Partner.

TrionX operates a full-service Facility Maintenance division to deliver expert-level commercial field services. Our team is structured to meet the demands of national facility programs and multi-site rollouts.

From lighting issues to plumbing repairs, glass replacements to refrigeration service, TrionX keeps your business running smoothly. We handle work orders, meet SLA requirements, provide thorough documentation, and maintain full vendor compliance.

  • Commercial electrical repairs & troubleshooting
  • Commercial lighting repairs, upgrades & LED retrofits
  • Plumbing fixture replacements & leak repairs
  • Storefront & commercial glass repair & replacement
  • Commercial refrigeration maintenance & repair
  • Door hardware & general repairs
  • Ceiling tile & drywall patching
  • Store resets & minor build-outs

We understand that downtime costs money. Our team works efficiently, communicates clearly, and leaves your space clean and operational.
